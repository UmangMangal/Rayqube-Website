Strategically Designed Maps

We design virtual maps that keep the user and the objectives of the event in mind and allow for easy access of the most relevant resources. We make sure that your virtual event map creates an impactful virtual experience where your guests can come together to learn, network, and be entertained.

Online Games

Virtual event games are a great way to maintain your attendee's attention to keep them lingering just a bit longer so you can entice them again! Not to mention, attendees will likely attend future events if they had fun the last time.

Virtual Networking Lounges

Allow your attendees the luxury to have deeper private conversations through written or video conversations. Virtual networking lounges are a great way to facilitate new collaborations, sponsorship agreements, and new business.

Interactive Polls

Engage your participants, capture their views and make everyone feel more connected with interactive polls, Q&As, quizzes, surveys, and word clouds. We turn your one-way presentations into engaging conversations so you can 'get your audiences' feedback in real time.

Private/Public Chats

Add public and private chatrooms for your next virtual event to create a community experience and further increase engagement. You can foster a comfortable environment and a sense of community and connection for your event attendees through private and public chats.

Studio On Demand

We offer boutique services that allow you to access your target audience from a live studio setup to keep your remote audience engaged and leave them wanting more. We use a green screen to create a highly immersive virtual landscape that makes statistics, keynote presentations, and product launches as visually rich as they are informative.
